    What We Offer

    Services Tailored for Greener Value Chains

    From sustainable sourcing and EUDR compliance to climate-smart agriculture training, Ngite Space offers a comprehensive suite of services designed to make agricultural value chains more sustainable, traceable, and climate-resilient.

    We work with farmers, traders, processors, and international buyers to bridge the gap between responsible production and conscious consumption.

    Our Services

    How We Help

    We operate across the full spectrum of sustainable agriculture, climate action, and responsible trade — delivering impact from the farm gate to international markets.

    01

    Sustainable Sourcing

    Responsibly sourcing coffee and cocoa with full traceability — connecting ethical buyers to verified Ugandan producers.

    02

    Buyer Representation

    Coffee and cocoa buyer representation — helping foreign companies and buyers sustainably identify and source quality commodities from Uganda.

    03

    EUDR Due Diligence

    Supporting companies in meeting EU Deforestation Regulation requirements through documentation, GIS mapping and comprehensive risk assessment.

    04

    GIS Services

    Geographic Information System services for farm mapping, land-use monitoring, and spatial analysis to support traceability and compliance.

    05

    Quality Assurance

    Third party quality assurance services ensuring coffee and cocoa products meet international standards for export and trade.

    06

    Climate Smart Agriculture

    Training smallholder farmers on climate-smart agriculture practices including irrigation, soil conservation, and agroforestry techniques.

    07

    Cleaner Production Training

    Cleaner production and value addition trainings for agro-processing companies to reduce environmental impact while improving output quality.

    08

    Carbon Accounting

    Carbon footprint accounting and environmental auditing for agro-processing firms — helping companies discover and manage their environmental impact.

    09

    Waste Disposal & Recycling

    Responsible waste disposal and recycling using the Cradle to Cradle principle — making waste a raw material for new products through innovation.

    10

    Climate Advocacy

    Climate change education, public campaigns and policy advocacy to drive systemic change in coffee and cocoa value chains.

    11

    Food Security Training

    Food security and post-harvest loss management trainings to help farming communities build resilience and reduce waste.

    12

    Climate Research

    Climate-focused research generating evidence-based insights to inform sustainable practices across agricultural value chains.

    Our Products

    Premium Products

    We grow, process, and trade premium quality coffee and cocoa — responsibly sourced from Ugandan farming communities.

    Agribusiness

    Coffee & Cocoa Trading

    We grow and process coffee and cocoa from our farms, buy from smallholder farmers in communities across Uganda. Our products are responsibly sourced and geared to be exemplary in the agribusiness-climate change relationship. We sell premium quality to buyers who understand that responsible sourcing drives sustainable value chains.

    Innovation

    Biochar & Green Charcoal

    We produce biochar from solid organic waste, used as a soil feed and fertilizer. Biochar improves soil health and fertility, with research showing its potential in sequestering and capturing carbon from the atmosphere — reducing greenhouse gases while empowering farmers with better crop yields.
