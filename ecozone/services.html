Products

1. Coffee and cocoa buying and selling

We grow and process coffee and cocoa from our farms, buy from small holder farmers in the coffee and cocoa farming communities around Uganda. Our cocoa and coffee products are responsibly sourced and geared to be exemplary in the agribusiness-climate change relationships. We sell coffee and cocoa of premium quality to buyers who understand that responsible sourcing is a key drive to sustainable value chains and development.

2. Biochar and green charcoal

We make biochar from solid organic waste which we use as a soil feed and /or fertilizer. Biochar has showed potential to improve soil health and fertility and farmers employing it on their farms have attested to the amazing results. Research has shown that Biochar plays a role in the sequestration and capture of carbon from the atmosphere into the soil thus reducing on Green House Gases in the atmosphere.
