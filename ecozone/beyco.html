What is BEYCO

The First Independent Coffee & Cocoa Trading Platform

In Partnership with Progreso Foundation · Uganda

BEYCO is both a web-based and mobile digital traceability platform developed by the Progreso Foundation and implemented in partnership with Ngite Space Limited in Uganda to support producer organisations, exporters and other stakeholders.

It digitises the coffee and cocoa value chains at an affordable cost. It is the first independent digital coffee and cocoa trading platform that allows farmers and producer organisations to have full ownership over their data.

BEYCO uses Blockchain technology to empower supply chain parties — creating a future where every bean contributes to a better world.

Supported value chains:

Coffee Arabica & Robusta · Kasese, Bundibugyo
Cocoa Fine Flavour Cocoa · Western Uganda

Why BEYCO

Six Ways BEYCO Strengthens Your Position

BEYCO aims to strengthen the market position of every actor in the value chain — from smallholder farmers to international buyers.

01

Farmer Registration & Management +

Register and manage complete farmer data including profiles, production volumes and delivery records — all digitally, all in one place.

02

EUDR Deforestation Risk Analysis +

Built-in tools that generate the deforestation risk analysis and compliance documentation required under the EU Deforestation Regulation.

03

Full Supply Chain Traceability +

Farmer and supply chain stories attached to every product at every step — traceable right down to the individual farm level via the Beyco Farmer App.

04

Cost-Effective EUDR Solution +

The most affordable solution on the market for achieving and demonstrating full EU Deforestation Regulation compliance without enterprise-level costs.

05

Direct Buyer Connections +

Connect producer organisations directly with international buyers through the integrated digital trading platform — removing unnecessary intermediaries.

06

Real-Time Trade & Market Data +

Access live pricing, trade flows, and market intelligence to make informed sourcing and selling decisions from a position of knowledge and strength.
