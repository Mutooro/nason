Who we are

Ngite Space Limited is an agro-processing and climate change focused company registered under the laws of Uganda and is dedicated to fostering sustainable food production and Responsible trade in the era of climate change. Our business strategy bridges the gap between producers, traders, consumers and climate change Activists in the world's food system. We foster collaborations between those who understand the need for responsible production, sourcing, processing and consumption.

At Ngite Space, we therefore champion cleaner production technologies to guarantee greener value chains for our priority crops; coffee and cocoa. We develop and promote products and services that focus on reducing the environmental impact of agricultural value chains yet ensuring sustainable food security. We also help foreign companies/buyers to sustainably indentify and source commondities or crops.

Our priority crops and value chains include:
1. Coffee
2. Cocoa

Through collective action, collaborations and community driven initiatives, we strive to create a more sustainable and equitable coffee and cocoa value chains.

Our Mission

Building climate resilience and adaptive capacity in the world's food system and trade, mainly coffee and cocoa value chains.


Our Vision

Inclusive, life changing and sustainable coffee and cocoa value chains emanating from our environmental action in the agro food system.


Our Values

  1. Sustainability
  2. Collaboration
  3. Trust
  4. Accountability

Our Key Objectives

To be the world's leading supplier of sustainable and Responsibly sourced coffee and cocoa.

To promote cleaner production technologies in the agro-food systems and farmer's daily life.

To support communities most vulnerable to climate change, helping them in areas of climate change adaption and resilience.

To conserve and restore natural ecosystems that play a vital role in climate regulation and biodiversity.

To embark on advocacy, research and public education on issues of climate change and sustainable agriculture in the coffee and cocoa value chains.

To carry-on the business of training farmers on climate smart, post harvest and value addition technologies.

FAQ

Have Any Questions?

1. What is climate change?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts and alterations in temperature, weather patterns, and environmental conditions, largely caused by human activities like burning fossil fuels and deforestation.

2. What causes climate change?

The primary cause is the increase in greenhouse gases, such as carbon dioxide, methane, and nitrous oxide, in the atmosphere. These gases trap heat, leading to global warming and climate disruption.

3. What are greenhouse gases?

Greenhouse gases are gases in the Earth's atmosphere that trap heat, contributing to global warming. The most common are carbon dioxide (CO), methane (CH), and nitrous oxide (NO).

4. How can we advocate for climate action?

Advocacy can include educating others, supporting policies for renewable energy, reducing emissions, participating in climate strikes, supporting climate-focused organizations, and holding governments and corporations accountable.
