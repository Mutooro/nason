Message from our ED

Ngite Space is a youth led Non-profit Organization dedicated to combating climate change for sustainable development.
Our work focuses on the following key thematic areas:
1. Climate Change Mitigation and Adaptation
2. Food Security and Community Nutrition
3. Clean water, Air and Energy
4. Gender Mainstreaming
5. Community Health (With focus on the impacts of climate change)

FAQ

Have Any Questions?

1. What is climate change?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts and alterations in temperature, weather patterns, and environmental conditions, largely caused by human activities like burning fossil fuels and deforestation.

2. What causes climate change?

The primary cause is the increase in greenhouse gases, such as carbon dioxide, methane, and nitrous oxide, in the atmosphere. These gases trap heat, leading to global warming and climate disruption.

3. What are greenhouse gases?

Greenhouse gases are gases in the Earth's atmosphere that trap heat, contributing to global warming. The most common are carbon dioxide (CO₂), methane (CH₄), and nitrous oxide (N₂O).

4. How can we advocate for climate action?

Advocacy can include educating others, supporting policies for renewable energy, reducing emissions, participating in climate strikes, supporting climate-focused organizations, and holding governments and corporations accountable.
