Who we are

Ngite Space Limited is an agro-processing and climate change focused company registered under the laws of Uganda registration number: 80034879454906 and is dedicated to fostering sustainable food production and Responsible trade in the era of climate change. Our business strategy bridges the gap between producers, traders, consumers and climate change Activists in the world's food system. We foster collaborations between those who understand the need for responsible production, sourcing, processing and consumption.

At Ngite Space, we therefore champion cleaner production technologies to guarantee greener value chains for our priority crops; coffee and cocoa. We develop and promote products and services that focus on reducing the environmental impact of agricultural value chains yet ensuring sustainable food security. We also help foreign companies/buyers to sustainably indentify and source commondities or crops.

Our priority crops and value chains include:
1. Coffee
2. Cocoa

Through collective action, collaborations and community driven initiatives, we strive to create a more sustainable and equitable coffee and cocoa value chains.

Our Mission

Building climate resilience and adaptive capacity in the world's food system and trade, mainly coffee and cocoa value chains.


Our Vision

Inclusive, life changing and sustainable coffee and cocoa value chains emanating from our environmental action in the agro food system.


Our Values

  2. Collaboration

  4. Accountability
