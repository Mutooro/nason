About Ngite Space

Our Story

Who We Are

Reg. No. 80034879454906 · Uganda

Ngite Space Limited is an agro-processing and climate change focused company registered under the laws of Uganda, dedicated to fostering sustainable food production and responsible trade in the era of climate change.

Our business strategy bridges the gap between producers, traders, consumers and climate change activists in the world's food system. We foster collaborations between those who understand the need for responsible production, sourcing, processing and consumption.

At Ngite Space, we champion cleaner production technologies to guarantee greener value chains. We develop and promote products and services that focus on reducing the environmental impact of agricultural value chains, while ensuring sustainable food security. We also help foreign companies and buyers to sustainably identify and source commodities.

Our priority crops and value chains:

Coffee Arabica & Robusta · Kasese, Bundibugyo
Cocoa Fine Flavour Cocoa · Western Uganda

Through collective action, collaborations and community-driven initiatives, we strive to create more sustainable and equitable coffee and cocoa value chains.

Our Work

What We Do

We operate across the full spectrum of sustainable agriculture and climate action — from the farm gate to international markets.

01

Sustainable Sourcing +

Responsibly sourcing coffee and cocoa with full traceability — connecting ethical buyers to verified Ugandan producers.

02

EUDR Compliance +

Supporting companies in meeting EU Deforestation Regulation requirements through documentation, GIS mapping and risk assessment.

03

Carbon Accounting +

Helping companies discover and manage their environmental impact through rigorous carbon accounting and sustainability reporting.

04

Farmer Training +

Training smallholder farmers on climate-smart agriculture, post-harvest handling and value addition technologies.

05

Climate Adaptation +

Supporting vulnerable farming communities with climate adaptation strategies, resilience building and ecosystem restoration.

06

Advocacy & Research +

Conducting research and public education on climate change and sustainable agriculture in coffee and cocoa value chains.
